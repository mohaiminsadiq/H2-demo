User Dataset Format Instructions

The uploaded dataset must be in a particular format for the system to detect and mitigate bias properly. Different information must be present in the dataset depending on the method the user intends on using to detect and mitigate bias.

SHAP

For using SHAP, a snippet of the dataset format is shown below:


  • The 'id' column is simply the ordered index of the instances.
  • The 'shap' column is for the shap score of the relevant protected attribute of the instance.
  • The 'label' column is the binary ground truth label. For this system we follow the convention of 1 being the positive outcome, e.g. high income or low recividism score, and 0 being the negative outcome, e.g. low income or high recividism score.
  • The 'group' column is the binary label for the protected attribute. For this system we follow the convention of 1 being the disadvantaged group and 0 being the advantaged group. For example, for COMPAS, African Americans are labeled 1, while Caucasians are labeled 0.
  • The 'prediction' column is the probability of belonging to label 1, output by the biased model.

Burden
